Introduction

Bouskoura District is located about twenty kilometers south of Casablanca.

Administrative Location

Bouskoura is located about twenty kilometers south of Casablanca. It is called by this name because it is located near the forest of Bouskoura which is one of the great forests of Morocco and which attracts a large number of Casablancais, on weekends and on public holidays.

Pollution

The World Wildlife Fund report indicates that the Bouskoura region is the most polluted in Morocco. The Bouskoura wadi, which was an aquatic environment where many animals lived (dozens of tiger's species, lions, dogs, cats, etc.) is seriously polluted by the dumping of industrial waste from surrounding factories.
