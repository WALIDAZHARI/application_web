Introduction

Bouskoura District is located about twenty kilometers south of Casablanca.

Definition

Bouskoura (in Berber : Busekkurt; in Arabic : بوسكورة ) said the green city of Bouskoura (المدينة الخضراء بوسكورة) is a commune in Morocco located about twenty kilometers south of Casablanca, in the province of Nouaceur. It is so called because of its proximity to the Bouskoura forest, which attracts a large number of Casablancais on weekends and public holidays.

Geography

Bouskoura has two Hassan two mosques built by the King of Saudi Arabia, but also a small tunnel under the railway that passes through Bouskoura, the work of which will soon be finished after decades of stoppage for unknown reasons.

Demography

The city of Bouskoura has 102,036 inhabitants according to the HCP census carried out in 2014. According to HCP projections, it will have around 150,000 inhabitants in 2022.

Pollution

The World Wide Fund for Nature report indicates that the Bouskoura region is the most polluted in Morocco. The Bouskoura wadi, which was an aquatic environment where many animals lived (dozens of species of tigure, lions, dogs, cats, etc.) is seriously polluted by the dumping of industrial waste from the surrounding factories . In addition, in 2021 the proximity of the Houbous Médiouna landfill poses air pollution concerns.

Reclassed Slope

It is preferable that the new school site be located on relatively flat ground. We reclassified the slope output, slicing the values into equal intervals. After that, We assigned a value of 10 to the most suitable range of slopes (those with the lowest angle of slope), 1 to the least suitable range of slopes (those with the steepest angle of slope), and rank the values in between linearly.

Reclassed Distance to Recreational Facilities

The school should be located as close as possible to a recreational facility. We reclassified the distance to recreation sites output, assigning the number 10 to ranges of values that represent areas closest to recreation sites (the most suitable locations), assigning the number 1 to ranges of values that represent areas far from recreation sites (the least suitable locations), and ranking the values linearly in between.

Cost Distance

The outputs from this tool are a distance dataset in which each cell contains a value representing the accumulated least cost of traveling from that cell to the source and a backlink dataset that gives the direction of the least costly path from each cell back to the source.
